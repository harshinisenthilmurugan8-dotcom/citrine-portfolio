# Why I Write — A Journey From Impulse to Purpose

I've never been someone who could fit into just one box. Ever since I was a child, I wanted to try everything — learn everything, become everything that inspired me. Surprisingly, life somehow allowed me to do exactly that.

## The Impulse

It was September 1, 2025. A completely random day. I had no plan, no outline, no burning desire to "become an author." I just… started typing. What I thought would be a casual experiment turned into my first completed novel in just two and a half days.

Since then, I haven't stopped writing.

## The Discovery

Writing, for me, isn't just about putting words on a page. It's about capturing moments, emotions, and truths that we often overlook in our daily lives. When I wrote *Love Ka Jugaad*, I wasn't just writing a rom-com — I was writing about the beautiful chaos of Indian weddings, the tension between tradition and modern love, and the courage it takes to follow your heart.

## Synesthesia and Creativity

I also experience synesthesia — a neurological condition where senses overlap in unique ways. For me, sounds, emotions, words, and visuals often connect together creatively. This deeply influences the way I write, imagine, and create art.

> "At the heart of everything I do, I simply want to create things that make people feel something."

## What's Next

I've written multiple books, published two so far, and started my own publishing journey. The dream? To help more writers bring meaningful stories into the world.

Stay tuned. The best is yet to come.
